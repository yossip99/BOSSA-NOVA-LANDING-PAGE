About Bossa Nova

Bossa Nova emerged in the late 1950s, combining soft samba rhythms with jazz harmony. Its gentle guitar patterns and intimate vocals create a calm, sophisticated sound loved around the world.

Featured Bossa Nova Artists

João Gilberto

Pioneer of Bossa Nova, known for soft vocals and the iconic guitar rhythm that shaped the genre.

Antônio Carlos Jobim

Legendary composer and pianist behind classics like "The Girl from Ipanema" and many timeless standards.

Astrud Gilberto

Internationally recognized voice whose elegant delivery helped bring Bossa Nova to global audiences.

Stan Getz

Influential jazz saxophonist whose collaborations blended cool jazz and Bossa Nova into a worldwide sound.
